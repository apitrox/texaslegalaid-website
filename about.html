About Texas Legal Aid

We believe everyone deserves access to justice, regardless of income. Our mission is to connect those in need with free legal services.

Our Mission

Texas Legal Aid was created to bridge the justice gap — the divide between those who can afford legal help and those who cannot.

Every year, millions of Americans face legal challenges without representation. Evictions, custody battles, immigration cases, domestic violence — these life-altering situations often go unaddressed because people don't know where to turn for help.

We're changing that. By partnering with 19+ legal aid organizations across Texas, we've created a single point of entry for those seeking free legal assistance. Our platform connects people with the right resources quickly and efficiently.
